Steam Chest Prices

Steam Chest Prices is a web application designed for monitoring the prices of CS2 (Counter-Strike 2) weapon cases on the Steam Market. Users can view historical price charts for each case and create an account to track their own collection. By adding owned cases to their profile, users can automatically calculate the total value of their inventory and see daily price changes.
